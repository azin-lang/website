Today, I have some unfortunate news to share.

Alien (also known as just.another.alien) has decided to step down as a founder of Azin. After discussing it, he felt that the project's direction no longer aligned with what he wanted to work on, so he has chosen to move on and will no longer be actively contributing to Azin.

Instead, he wants to focus on his own game, [Blackline FC](https://blackline-fc.dev).

I'll be updating the founders page shortly to reflect this change.

I'd like to thank Alien for everything he contributed during Azin's early days, and I wish him the best of luck with Blackline FC and his future projects.
